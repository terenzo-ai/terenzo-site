ROI summary

The money, year one.

Implementation (one-time)
$58K
Annual savings
$128K
Year-one ROI
36%
Year-two ROI
256%

Annual savings = hours saved per week × people × loaded hourly rate × 52. Here: AP matching $32K + support drafting $39K + reporting pack $57K = $128K. Year-one cost = $58K implementation + $36K maintenance ($3K/mo). ROI = (savings − cost) ÷ cost.
