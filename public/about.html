About

The firm a CFO hires when the operating model has outgrown the spreadsheets.

Terenzo is an AI and technology operations consulting firm for mid-market companies, 200 to 2000 employees. We work directly with CFOs and COOs. We are senior, calm, and accountable: in and out with the mess sorted, not a year-long engagement that never ends. The name comes from San Terenzo, a village on the Ligurian coast of Italy.
