How much does AI consulting cost for a mid-market company?

An AI operations audit runs $5,000 to $10,000 for a small company and $25,000 to $50,000 for a mid-market company. Implementation is quoted after the audit and delivered through vetted partners. A maintenance retainer, typically $2,000 to $5,000 a month, keeps the work current.

What is an AI operations audit?

A two-week diagnostic that maps where AI and better systems will move your numbers, ranks the opportunities by impact and effort, and hands you a phased roadmap with year-one ROI. See the AI audit page.

Do we need clean data or a data team before we start?

No. The audit works with the business you have today. We find the opportunities in your current workflows and tell you what, if anything, needs to be in place before each one.

Should we hire an AI consultant or build the capability internally?

Start with a diagnosis. An audit is faster and cheaper than a hire, and it hands your team a ranked plan they can run. If the roadmap justifies a full-time role later, you will know exactly what to hire for.

Do you implement the AI, or just advise?

We diagnose and design. Implementation is delivered through vetted partners and quoted after the audit. Keeping the recommendation separate from the build keeps our advice honest.

How do you calculate the ROI in the roadmap?

One auditable formula: hours saved per week, times people, times loaded hourly rate, times 52, set against the full cost of implementation and maintenance. Every input traces to a named interview; vendor benchmarks are never used. The full method is public: how we measure ROI, and a sample readout shows it applied, with the arithmetic on the slide.

How fast will we see results?

The roadmap front-loads quick wins, the high-impact and low-effort changes, for the first 30 to 90 days. Bigger builds come in months four to eight.

What kind of companies do you work with?

Mid-market companies, 200 to 2000 employees, where a CFO or COO owns the operating number. We also run a smaller audit, $5,000 to $10,000, for companies below that range. We are the strongest fit for B2B SaaS, cloud and managed service providers, and legal, tax, and compliance-heavy firms.

Is our data safe? What about security?

We work on process and systems, and we advise on where AI fits. We do not move production data or stand up tools without your security review. Credentials and secrets stay with you.

What makes Terenzo different from other AI consultants?

We sell diagnosis, not tools. No AI theatre, no year-long engagement. Senior operators who sort the mess and hand back systems your team can run without us.

How do we get started?

Book a call. Tell us where the operating model is straining, and we will tell you whether an audit is the right next step and what it would find.
